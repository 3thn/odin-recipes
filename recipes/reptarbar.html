Reptar Bar Recipe

This marshmellow filled chocolate bar is perfect for any occasion. If the Rugrats love it so will you!

Ingredients

  • Marshmellow Fluff
  • Green Food Coloring
  • Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • Chcoloate Bar Mold
  • Heavy Cream
  • Caramel Candies
  • Grahmcracker Crumbs

Steps:

  1. Put your chocolate chips in a suacepan over medium heat, until creamy consistency is reached.
  2. Microwave fluff for 15 seconds and add food coloring.
  3. Melt down caramels and add heavy creaam.
  4. Layer your chocolate, green fluff, heavy cream/caramel mixture and Grahmcrackers in mold.
  5. Place in fridge until solid.
  6. Enjoy!
