Michealangelo's Pizza Recipe

Michealangelo from Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles favorite pizza recipie.

Ingredients

  • Anchovy Sauce
  • Strawberries
  • Pizza Dough
  • Rolling Pin

Steps:

  1. First pre-heat your oven to 400.
  2. Roll out your pizza dough.
  3. Place your anchovy sauce on the pizza.
  4. Cut up your strawberries and place on the pizza.
  5. Cook the pizza for 35 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
