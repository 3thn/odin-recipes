Krabby Patty Recipe

This delightful burger, invented by Mr.Krabs himself, is full of flavor. Krab's uses his not so secret formula to perfect this burger recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Barnacle Shavings
  • Salt
  • Tumeric
  • Love
  • The "secret sauce"
  • Chum
  • Buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Sea Pickles
  • Relish
  • Ground Sea Cow
  • Tartar Suce
  • Sea lettuce

Steps:

  1. Turn on grill to high and grab your trusty spatuala.
  2. Throw your ground sea cow patty on the grill for 2 minutes, flip halfway.
  3. Place ketchup, mustard, pickles, luttuce and tartar sauce on the bun.
  4. Add your burger and cheese to the bun.
  5. Add any additional toppings.
  6. Enjoy your krabby patty!
